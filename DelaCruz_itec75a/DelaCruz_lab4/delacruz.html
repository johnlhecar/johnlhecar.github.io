Top 5 budget gaming phones of 2021

More specs less money? check this out


5

Realme C15


Price: PHP 6,490

If you’re looking for an entry-level gaming phone packed with powerful features, the Realme C15 is for you. While it’s affordable, you already get a gaming device equipped with Helio G35 octa-core processor with HyperEngine technology, which can handle multitasking and heavy gaming with ease.

Realme C15 Specs:

Chipset: MediaTek Helio G35

GPU: PowerVR GE8320

OS: Android 10, Realme UI

RAM: 4GB

Storage: 128GB

Display: 6.5” HD + mini-drop full screen


4

Vivo Y30


Price: PHP 8,999

If you’re looking for the best gaming phone under PHP 10K in the Philippines, the Vivo Y30 is something that you should add to your list. With its large battery capacity of 5,000mAH, you get up to 9 hours of gameplay (though this is for single-app operations only).

Vivo Y30 Specs:

Chipset: MediaTek MT6765 Helio P35

GPU: PowerVR GE8320

OS: Android 10, Funtouch 10.0

RAM: 4GB

Storage: 128GB

Battery: 5,000mAh

Display: 6.47” HD display


3

Xiaomi POCO NFC X3


Price: PHP 12,990

Xiaomi is the maker of the Black Shark gaming phones. So, you can expect that the Chinese smartphone brand has kept gamers in mind while building this stunning mid-ranger. It runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G processor, which boasts strong graphics processing and excellent AI computing capabilities

Xiaomi POCO NFC X3 Specs:

Chipset: Qualcomm SM7150-AC Snapdragon 732G

GPU: Adreno 618

OS: Android 10, MIUI 12

RAM: 6GB

Storage: 128GB

Battery: 5,160mAh

Display: 6.67-inch Full High Definition (FHD)


2

Huawei Nova 7i


Price: PHP 12,999

In the last few years, HUAWEI phones are marketed as devices designed for amateur and professional photographers. But the brand’s smartphones are also loaded with powerful features that will surely delight gamers. One of the best entry-level options that you can get from this brand is the Nova 7i.

HUAWEI Nova Specs:

Chipset: HUAWEI Kirin 810

GPU: Mali-G52 MP6

OS: Android 10, EMUI

RAM: 8 GB

Storage: 128GB

Battery: 4,200mAh

Display: 6.4-inch HUAWEI Punch FullView Display


1

Samsung Galaxy A51


Price: PHP 14,990

Another well-rounded mid-ranger under the Samsung banner is the Galaxy A51. While it’s considered a lifestyle phone, it can take on some gaming challenges with its 8GB RAM. The phone runs on Exynos 9611, which may be considered quite dated. But still, it can run popular games smoothly.

Samsung Galaxy A51 Specs:

Chipset: Exynos 9611 octa-core processor

GPU: Mali-G72 MP3

OS: Android 10, which can be upgraded to Android 11, One UI 3.1

RAM: 8 GB

Storage: 128GB

Battery: 4,000mAh

Display: 6.5-inch Full HD + Super AMOLED
